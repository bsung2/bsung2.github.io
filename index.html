Intro

I am visiting assistant professor under Dave Morrison in the Department of Mathematics at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I received my PhD in Physics in 2022 under Jim Halverson and Emanuele Macrì at Northeastern University, where I was partially supported by an NSF graduate research fellowship.

I work at the intersection of algebraic geometry and string theory, with broad interests in physical mathematics, and I collaborate with both mathematicians and physicists. My current research focuses on string compactifications, Bridgeland stability conditions, and their interactions.
